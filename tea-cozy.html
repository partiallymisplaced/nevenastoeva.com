Tea Cozy

Identity

Challenge

The Tea Cozy is one of those places where you would get your portion of masterfully blended and rare imported teas - in bulk. And, if you have the time, you could take a seat and enjoy a quiet conversation with a friend over a cup of tea - the one you always wanted to try, just to make sure it's the one you want to take home. The friendly owners would be more than glad to give you helpful little tips or recommend something new - after all, they have created the unique blends themselves.

Solution

A distinct, playful logo was coined to convey the warm, cozy feeling of being in the store or just savouring a good cup of tea. Several cheerful patterns are there to give the customer a choice in which business card they'll take the same way they choose their tea. The custom stationery means to leave a lasting impression when the owners need to send a letter, and the customizable labels for the reusable pouches and tins have room to write down everything you need to know but you don't need to remember.
