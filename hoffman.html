Hoffman Paints

Packaging Design

Challenge

Hoffman High Quality Paints is a fairly new, family owned paint company, makers of environmentally friendly paint products. They are seeking to establish themselves on the market and reach the hearts of homeowners who very much enjoy getting their hands dirty and share Hoffman's care for the environment while not compromising on refreshing the walls of their living room or finally remodeling that mother-in-law unit that's needed it for some ten years now.

Solution

First came the simple, yet sturdy, reliable and memorable wordmark. Then, the line of products was dressed in a simple, approachable and straight-to-the-point package speaking of the joy of seeing a room reborn, gleaming like new with a fresh coat of paint. A paint that is also safe, both for the environment and for you and yours. A good amount of attention was paid to all the things you need to know before you get started on your project.
