Northwest Passage

Web Design

Challenge

Northwest Passage is an Arts and Culture online magazine focused on the cultural life in the Pacific Northwest, namely Washington, Oregon and British Columbia. It aims to provide reviews, commentary and listings/event info for a broad contemporary art scene. The members of its diverse audience need to easily find their way though the vast amount of topics the magazine covers and stay informed of current events in their own state while having an easy access to see what's happening in the other two.

Solution

All the topics that the owners requested to be included were organized though card-sorting and laid out in a two-tier navigation menu. The front page highlights and combines the most important sections. Each region is easily recognized through a distinctive highlight color and region-specific information is loaded while the simple, grid based layout remains consistent. view site
