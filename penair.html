PenAir

Identity

Challenge

PenAir is one of the oldest and most reliable Alaskan commuter airlines and, until 2010, it was also the largest. After the merge of several smaller airlines into what is now Era Alaska, PenAir is now second largest. With Era being featured on Discovery's Flying Wild Alaska, PenAir noticed decline in popularity and decided that they needed to update their brand identity and make a bold statement about their position on the market.

Solution

The proposed redesign began with a major revision of the logo - distilling the class and reliability of the company and the freedom they provide with their flights and combining them into a memorable mark. A graphic standarts manual was drafted to outline the new identity of the brand and its application in business cards, stationery, air-crafts, luggage tags, tickets and other ephemera.
