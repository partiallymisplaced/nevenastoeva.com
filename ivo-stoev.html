Ivo Stoev

Identity

Challenge

Ivo is an experienced front and back end developer, and origami enthusiast. He recently moved to Seattle and needed a comprehensive resume and a simple, engaging portfolio to present the projects he has worked on when approaching (or being approached) by potential employers. While his skills are in the realm of building and maintaining databases, he did not want to compromise on the appearance of his identity.

Solution

For the sake of keeping it simple, the one-page interactive website uses jQuery to display each different section and present all necessary information. It also relies on embedded fonts and CSS3 to achieve visual richness while keeping the content easy to update when needed. A set of MiniCards by moo.com is most fitting to extend the playful nature of the identity and Ivo's personality. view site
