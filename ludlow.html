Mrs. Ludlow's Tea

Packaging Design

Challenge

Mrs. Ludlow has long known that a good cup of tea can help your daily worries go away. Even more, she knows which herb you need to soothe your throat, clear your mind or help you fall asleep. When her descendants decided to share her wisdom with people looking for natural ways to maintain their good health, their main goal was to establish a trustworthy brand that would stand out with elegance and a clear message about the benefits provided by their products.

Solution

An elegant wordmark embellished with golden fleurons tells a story of a company with rich history and traditions. Inspiration was sought in old medicinal packaging and even older botanical journals. With titles and descriptions in memorable typefaces and an exquisite texture of golden fleurons, each tea variety would receive an unique, equally elegant botanical illustration. Even when seen away from the box, each tea sachet would still carry the original Mrs. Ludlow feel.
