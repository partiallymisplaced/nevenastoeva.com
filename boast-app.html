Boast App

UI/UX Design

Challenge

Boast is a new iPhone app that makes it easy for you to share your recent purchases with your friends. Scan the barcode of the pair of shoes you just got, and you're two steps away from letting everyone you know how excited you are about your purchase. And if you decide to let the app use your current location, you'll get access to special offers by nearby businesses.

Solution

Together with Joe Brokken, we researched the personality and habits of our potential users and worked to come up with an solution that is fun, simple and easy to navigate. We evaluated the workflow and came up with a walktrough covering several user scenarios. The documentation we compiled is available here.
